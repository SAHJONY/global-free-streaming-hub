Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Our Commitment to Privacy

Front Desk Agents is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle your information when you use our self-hosted WhatsApp CRM platform.

Self-Hosted Architecture

Front Desk Agents is designed to be self-hosted on your own infrastructure. Your customer data, messages, and conversations remain entirely within your control. We do not have access to your data stored on your servers.

Information We Collect

For our cloud-hosted customers, we collect only the minimum information necessary to provide our service: account credentials, billing information, and usage metadata for service improvement.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, please reach out at privacy@frontdeskagents.com.
