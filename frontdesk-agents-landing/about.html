About Front Desk Agents

We're on a mission to make WhatsApp the most powerful sales channel for businesses of every size.

Our Story

Front Desk Agents was born from a simple frustration: too many businesses were losing leads because WhatsApp wasn't built for team collaboration. Messages got lost, follow-ups fell through the cracks, and there was no way to track what was working.

We built Front Desk Agents to solve that — a CRM designed specifically for WhatsApp. One shared inbox. Smart automations. Full visibility into every conversation. All self-hosted so you stay in control of your data.

What We Believe

🔒 Data Privacy First

Your customer data belongs to you, not us. Self-hosting means complete control over privacy and compliance.

⚡ Speed Matters

Every second counts in sales. We build tools that help your team respond faster and close more deals.

🤝 Human-First Automation

Automation should enhance human connection, not replace it. We automate the busywork so you can focus on relationships.

🌍 Accessible to All

Whether you're a solo founder or a 100-person team, our tools scale with you at a fair price.
