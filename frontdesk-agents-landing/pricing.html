Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans at any time?

Yes! Upgrade or downgrade anytime. Your data and settings stay intact.

Is self-hosting really free?

The Starter plan is free forever when self-hosted. You only pay for your own infrastructure.

Do I need a WhatsApp Business account?

Yes, WhatsApp Business API access is required. We'll help you set it up during onboarding.

What kind of support do you offer?

Starter includes community support. Professional adds email support. Enterprise gets a dedicated account manager.
